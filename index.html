Welcome to Clear Skies, an astronomy blog!

Did you know that you can see a galaxy 2.5 million light-years away with your naked eyes? Amateur astronomy is an amazing hobby that lets you see backward in time and explore the many cosmic wonders of our Universe. All you gotta do is to look up!

Contrary to what we might think, a lot of the celestial delights can be spotted with our naked eyes or even a pair of small binoculars. The aim of this blog is to get you excited about amateur astronomy as well as to keep a digital record of all my observations of the night sky.
