19th Mar 2011

Saturday, 6:40 pm

Today is the Super Full Moon. It happened the last time about 20 or 21 years ago. It is when the Moon will be the closest to the Earth (at Perigee). It will be 14% bigger than when it reaches perigee this time around. The Moon was also red in color today.
