29th November 2020

Sunday, 7:10 pm to 7:55 pm

Observed Jupiter and Saturn and took pictures. I was able to starhop to the location of M75 from Saturn. Its very near. I did confirm the location of the cluster with the positions of the nearby asterisms. I was not able to see the cluster however. It is a magnitude 9 object and I will possibly try again on a moonless night now that I know the position well.
