SaturdaY, 7:30 pm

it has been so long after the last entry. I didn't get much time for stargazing due to my exams. April was so cloudy and I couldn't do much.

Today it is a cresent Moon. The sky is bright and full of stars. I don't have any maps though.

Anyway I found the Big and Little Dippers. The stars have moved a lot since my last observations. Last time, Orion was somewhere near the zenith. Now it is in the far West.
